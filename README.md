# Pineda-Sebastian-A3-Project
RED vs BLUE pong
Red Player (Left Paddle):

W – Move Up

S – Move Down

Blue paddle is controlled by an AI player

After every point, the ball waits patiently in the middle.
Press SPACE to launch it and begin the round
Keep the ball from getting past your paddle.
Each time the ball hits a paddle, it speeds up increasing its speed and difficulty to intercept.
First player to reach 5 points wins the match!
If you win, you’ll see a victory message!
Press R to start a new game whenever.
